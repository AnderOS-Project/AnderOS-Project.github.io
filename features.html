AnderOS Features

Explore the modular tools that power your system.

ModTone

A theme manager that lets you customize your OS look with modular packs. Designed for comfort-first styling and cross-OS expansion.

BitBoard

Your widget dashboard. Snap in system monitors, clocks, music controls, and more—fully modular and theme-synced.

Spec Sheet

A lightweight system monitor that shows CPU, RAM, and storage stats in a clean, founder-coded layout.

Andium

A modular browser project with slash commands, pack support, and future integration with AnderOS. Built for speed and customization.
